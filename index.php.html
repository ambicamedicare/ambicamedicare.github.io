H2O2 PLASMA

STERILIZER

Low Temperature H2O2 Plasma Sterilizer has the lowest cost of ownership for sterilizing heat & moisture sensitive equipment in CDDS, Respiratory Endoscopy, and Gastroenterology clinics.

ETO STERILIZER

Ethylene oxide (EtO or EO) sterilization plays an important role in an effective healthcare infection control program because of its reliability and gentleness.

STEAM STERILIZER

The widely used healthcare sterilant in the world steam effectively kills micro-organisms,works quickly.Steam uses both pressure & high temperature to achieve sterility.

DRY HEAT STERILIZER

Dry heat, as the name indicates, utilizes hot air that is either free from water vapour where this moisture plays a minimal role in the process of sterilization.

ETO SCRUBBER

The ETO scrubbing system is based on absorption of ethylene oxide and its subsequent hydration to ethylene glycol.

MLTI COLUMN PLANT

multi column distilled water plant designed to produce 100% pyrogen free. Multicolumn distilled water plant works on the principle of interstate heat exchanger

STEAM GENERATOR

A steam generator is a form of low water-content boiler, similar to a flash steam boiler.

PHARMASUTICAL TUNNEL

For depyrogenation & on line sterilization for washed vials & ampoules, stericlean hot laminar flow sterilization tunnel is used.

BUNG PROCESSOR

Processing of bungs (electrometric closures for drug containers) is of high concern since they play a significant role in the quality and life of the final product.

FOOD STERILIZER

The food Sterilizer uses electrolysis to eliminate harmful bacteria in everything from fruits, veggies, kitchen appliances.

PURE ETO STERILIZER

The Pressurised ETO is a small, simple to operate, table - top model for sterilization of linen,glass ware, syringes etc.which will operate with 100%(ETO gas Cartridges).
